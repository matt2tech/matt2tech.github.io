About Me

I was born in Winfield, Alabama and raised in Pontotoc, Mississippi. Friends and family describe me as intelligent, headstrong, and hard-working. At the age of thirteen, I helped build my grandmother's wonderful porch and scrapped metal ranging from tin to copper. In high school of my senior year, I worked at a shipping warehouse in Pontotoc for the online store Trendy Tree. Over the summer of 2018, I helped fix up my friend and her sisters' college home along with her dad and her brother. Upon graduating Pontotoc City High School in 2018, I ranked at the top of my class. During my senior year, I was led to Jesus Christ, accepted him, and now is the most important part of my life. I believe in doing what's right and staying true to one's integrity. I enjoy going to church, helping out around my church, playing video games, and coding. I grew up wanting to be a gaming developer and still want to build my own game. My dream is to raise a family and provide for them.
